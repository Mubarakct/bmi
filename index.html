About Us

Welcome to British Mount International pre-school, an initiative of BMIA (British Mount International Academy) under MES (Mount Education Society). Our mission is to provide high-quality education to children in a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment that builds a foundation for lifelong learning. At British Mount International pre-school, we address the growing demand for high-quality pre-school education in the wake of new National Education Policy. Our focus is on providing value education to children in a high-quality learning environment. We offer highly qualified and trained teachers in the Montessori education system, creating a wonderful self- learning atmosphere enabled with modern technologies. Our management team includes highly qualified academicians and educational professionals who have more than 12 years of presence in early education, higher education, and career development industry. Our curriculum is focused on value education to ensure the multi-dimensional growth of children, and our air-conditioned modern classrooms are equipped with innovative education technologies. Our highly interactive and supportive education system provides an international learning environment with trained teachers who have international experience. Our overarching goals are to provide a strong foundation for all-round development and lifelong learning, as well as to prepare children for school. We have specific objectives in place to achieve these goals, including creating a child-friendly environment where each child is valued and respected, enabling a sound foundation for good health, fostering effective communication skills, helping children become involved learners, and fostering a smooth transition from preschool to primary schools. We also work in partnership with parents and the community to enable each child to flourish. At British Mount International pre-school, we strive to provide an exceptional educational experience that helps prepare children for a successful future.

Our Programs

British Mount International pre-school offers a range of programs to cater to the diverse needs of children and their families. We follow the Montessori system of education, which emphasizes activity-based learning and encourages children to explore and learn at their own pace. Our highly qualified teachers are fluent in English, and they are dedicated to creating a nurturing and stimulating learning environment for all children.

Playgroup

Playgroup: Our playgroup program is designed for children between the ages of 18 months and 2 years. It is an ideal introduction to structured learning and is aimed at fostering independence and social skills.

Admissions

• Admission Process

The admissions process at British Mount International Academy Preschool is simple and straightforward. It involves filling out an admission application and a student skill test. The skill test is designed to assess the child's developmental level and help us understand their learning needs better.

• Enrollment Requirements

We welcome children of all nationalities and backgrounds who meet the age requirements for our different programs. The child must be at least 2 years and 6 months old for Play Group and up to 4 years old for Nursery. For LKG and UKG, the child's age must be between 4 and 6 years.

• Application Process

The application process starts on April 1 and ends on June 30. Please note that we have limited seats, and admissions are offered on a first-come, first-served basis. We encourage you to submit your application as early as possible to secure a seat for your child.
